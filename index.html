Welcome to Riyadh

Where A Traditional Past Meets A Modern Future.

History

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts, managed by the Municipality of Riyadh Region, headed by the mayor of Riyadh, and the Development Authority of Riyadh, chaired by the governor of the Province, Faisal bin Bandar Al Saud.

Culture

Riyadh has a rich cultural heritage, with numerous museums, galleries, and performance spaces. The city is home to historic landmarks and modern architectural wonders, showcasing a blend of tradition and innovation.

Entertainment

Riyadh has a growing entertainment scene with various events, festivals, and cultural exhibitions taking place throughout the year, providing diverse entertainment options for locals and tourists alike.
